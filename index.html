Our Story

A Tradition of Slow Fermentation & Real Ingredients

At Golden Crust Bakery, we believe that great bread takes time. We refuse to use commercial yeast shortcuts, instead relying on wild sourdough cultures nurtured over years.

Every loaf and pastry is hand-shaped, using only locally sourced organic flour from stoneground mills. It's a labor of love that you can taste in every single bite.
